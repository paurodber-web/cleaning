House Cleaning Cost in Melbourne: A Practical Pricing Guide

If you’re comparing house cleaning services in Melbourne, prices can vary quite a bit. Understanding what actually influences the cost will help you choose the right service without overpaying or missing important details.

Here’s a clear breakdown of how cleaning prices typically work.

Hourly rates vs fixed pricing

Some cleaning services charge by the hour, while others offer fixed quotes based on the size of the property and the work required.

Hourly rates can be a good option for smaller or occasional jobs where the scope is limited. Fixed pricing, on the other hand, gives you certainty upfront and is often preferred for regular cleaning or larger tasks.

What’s included in regular cleaning

Standard weekly or fortnightly cleaning usually covers the essentials needed to keep a home in good condition.

This typically includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping floors, cleaning bathrooms, and wiping down kitchen surfaces. The final price will depend on the number of bedrooms and bathrooms, as well as the overall condition of the home.

Why deep cleaning costs more

A deep clean goes beyond routine maintenance and requires more time and attention to detail.

It often includes cleaning inside appliances and cupboards, scrubbing grout, and tackling areas like skirting boards, blinds, and light fixtures. Because of the extra work involved, deep cleaning services are usually priced higher than standard visits.

End of lease and move-out cleaning

End of lease cleaning in Melbourne is typically more expensive because it needs to meet strict real estate standards.

These cleans are more detailed and may include additional services like carpet steam cleaning. The goal is to leave the property in a condition that helps secure the full bond return.

Optional extras that affect pricing

Many cleaning companies offer add-on services that are not included in a standard clean.

These can include oven cleaning, interior window cleaning, and balcony wash-downs. Adding these services will increase the total cost, so it’s always a good idea to check exactly what’s included in your quote.

What influences the final quote

Several factors can impact the price of a cleaning service. Property size is one of the biggest, but other things like ease of access, whether you have pets, and the current condition of the home also play a role. A well-maintained home is usually quicker and more affordable to clean than one that needs a full reset.

How to get the best value

The cheapest option isn’t always the best. It’s worth comparing quotes, reading reviews, and making sure the company is properly insured. A reliable cleaning service will deliver consistent results and save you time in the long run, which is where the real value comes from.

Plan your cleaning budget with confidence

Once you understand how pricing works, it becomes much easier to choose a service that fits your needs and your schedule. At the end of the day, hiring a cleaner isn’t just about the cost. It’s about the time and energy you get back, and the comfort of coming home to a space that’s properly looked after.

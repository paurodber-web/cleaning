Description

Our Deep Clean service goes far beyond the surface to tackle the dirt and grime that accumulates over time in hard-to-reach places. This intensive cleaning is ideal for seasonal refreshes or when your home needs extra attention. We meticulously scrub kitchen appliances, sanitize bathrooms from top to bottom, and clean those often-forgotten areas like baseboards and behind furniture. Our professional team uses specialized tools and powerful yet safe cleaning agents to ensure a hygienic and truly sparkling result. A deep clean restores your home to its best possible condition, providing a healthier environment for your family and a fresh start for your regular maintenance routine.

Included Tasks

  • Intensive scrubbing of shower floors and walls
  • Wiping of all skirting boards and doors
  • Detailed dusting of blinds and light fixtures
  • Sanitizing inside bathroom drawers and cabinets
  • Range hood and stovetop degreasing

Description

Our Move In / Out Clean service is specifically designed for those transitioning between homes. Whether you are moving into a new place and want it sanitized before your arrival, or you are moving out and need to ensure your deposit is returned, our team provides the deep scrub required for empty properties. We focus on the details that matter most during a handover, including cleaning inside cupboards, wiping down every surface, and ensuring bathrooms and kitchens are clinical. Our thorough approach removes the stress of cleaning from your moving day, allowing you to focus on your new beginning while we ensure your previous or future home is in pristine condition.

Included Tasks

  • Deep cleaning inside oven and rangehood
  • Sanitizing inside all kitchen drawers and cabinets
  • Detailed cleaning of all internal windows and tracks
  • Removal of cobwebs and dusting of all walls
  • Full interior cupboard cleaning in all rooms
